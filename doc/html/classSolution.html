it creates the matrix of the solutions evaluated on k evenly spaced points between zmin and zmax for the (n+1) first energy levels (up to the nth level)

the columns each matches one level of energy from the lowest to the highest and the rows each matches one point from zmin to zmax. The returned matrix is of size (k+1,n+1)
